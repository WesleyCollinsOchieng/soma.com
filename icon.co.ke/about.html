ABOUT US

1. Establishment

Universities are knowledge based Institutions, and are engines of economic growth and regional competitiveness. Universities spur innovation in close interaction with industry and other partners. Therefore, Universities are demanded to have competitive advantage in their areas of expertise and be able to promote leadership through research and innovation. Kisii University International Centre for Innovation Limited is registered under the Companies Act (Chapter 486, Laws of Kenya). It was incorporated in 2019. It provides Manufacturing, Consultancy and Partnerships, Medical Services, Agribusiness, Hospitality, Training and Other Commercial Initiatives with the University Resource Base. KICI is wholly owned Subsidiary by Kisii University and thus the resources available to KICI are primarily those of the University. It is governed by the Board of Directors, which is responsible for the general policy and strategic Direction of the Company.
