Commercialization of the University Intellectual Property

The Commercial Arm of the University promotes the University towards being a world-class University in advancement of academic excellence, research and social welfare, the enterprise promotes the University through implementing a Quality Management System (QMS) In accordance with ISO 9001:2015 Standard. KICI is committed to Inculcate entrepreneurial and best business culture through provision of Quality Agribusiness Products and Extension, Consultancy and Partnerships, Hospitality, Learning Resources, Medical and Financial Management Services that meet its customersâ€™ requirements and endeavor to exceed their expectation . The Management is committed to provide resources to comply with the requirements and continually improve the Quality Management System in line with the Standard. Management shall develop Quality Objectives derived from the Corporate Strategic Plan and the Service Charter. Management shall annually review these objectives for continuing suitability to improve the effectiveness of the Quality Management System. The management shall ensure that the Quality Policy is communicated and understood during Staff Induction and Training. All KICI Personnel are required to understand the Goals of the Company and apply the Quality Management System Documentation during products and service delivery. The Management shall continually monitor and annually review the Performance of the Quality Management System to ensure its Suitability, Adequacy and Effectiveness.
